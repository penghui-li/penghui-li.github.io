Research interests and background

My research area is geometric representation theory and geometric Langlands. Specific topics of interest include singular supports, Lie groups, higher categories, moduli stacks of vector bundles, character sheaves, Springer theory, Coxeter groups and Hecke algebras.

My papers and preprints are listed on my research page, and my CV is available here.

I completed my PhD at the University of California, Berkeley in 2016 under the guidance of David Nadler.
